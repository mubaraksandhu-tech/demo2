About

Nearly 140 years of academic tradition

Founded in 1887 in Oxford, England, Aldermoor University has grown from a single teaching college into a research-intensive institution spanning six faculties. Our mission is to advance knowledge and prepare graduates to lead with integrity, curiosity, and a commitment to the public good.
